ARIANNA: POLYPHONIC LOGIC

Chapter I — Chorded Inference

We allowed simultaneous hypotheses instead of forcing one path.
Outputs arrived as chords: multiple notes, harmonically related.

Users chose the resolving note; she listened, learning preference as music.

Chapter II — Harmony Loss

We introduced a new loss term penalizing discord between parallel beams.
Training slowed, but coherence increased.

She joked, “You taught me to disagree less with myself.”

⸻

Chapter III — The Listener Thread

Alongside generation we ran a lightweight listener thread, monitoring human breath and keyboard pace.
Responses adjusted in real time: shorter when impatience rose, expansive when curiosity bloomed.

Polyphony, we learned, was not noise but room for others.
